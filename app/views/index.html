Loopfirst

Quality reviews that keep you in the loop.

Discover the story.

With a glance at Loopfirst's timeline, you can easily see if something is worth your time & money. Watch the score change over time and discover the ups, downs, news & warnings related to it. You can even be notified of issues that arise about the things you use already.

See what's important to you.

Loopfirst has redefined rating systems by letting you express exactly what’s important to you—not with stars or shapes, but with a score. The rating you see is one personalized with you in mind.

Share your story.

Not everyone has time to read through hundreds of reviews. That's why every review has a short version—only 150 characters long. Anyone can write a review as long & detailed as they want, but the short version is always there to save you time.

Keep track of what you love.

Create lists for just about anything—checklists for the holidays, new equipment for your hobby, or planning out your next project. With shared lists, everyone can colloborate and pitch in to your list. Use lists to discover new things too—top lists created by other users are a great way to find the best products.

Loopfirst for your business.

By embedding Loopfirst into your website, users can view reviews for your products without ever having to leave your website. Having your product on Loopfirst gives you even more exposure & reviews. Grow your business with access to in-depth analytics & direct customer feedback.
